  1. What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG? For photographs.
  2. What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF? For simple animations and logos with flat colors.
  3. What is unique about the PNG format? Transparency. PNGs are high quality and are the best choice for images that need a clear, transparent background
  4. How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed? Use any photo editing app (like Photoshop, or a free online tool) to change the "Image Size" to the exact dimensions you need before you upload it to your webpage
  5. Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS? Because it slows down your website. If you resize with CSS, the browser still has to download the full, large image file first and then shrink it. By resizing the photo in an editor, you make the actual file smaller, so it downloads much faster.
