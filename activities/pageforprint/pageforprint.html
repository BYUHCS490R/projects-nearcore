Why is the viewport meta tag important?

It tells your phone's browser to fit the website to your small screen. Without it, the browser shows the huge desktop version, and you'd have to pinch and zoom constantly.

How can you adjust the CSS for printing?

You use a special rule in your CSS called @media print. This rule lets you write styles that only apply when someone prints the page, like hiding menus or changing fonts.

When would you want to adjust the CSS for printing?

You do it when you want the printed page to look clean. It's perfect for hiding things you don't need on paper, like website navigation, ads, and colorful backgrounds, just leaving the important content.
