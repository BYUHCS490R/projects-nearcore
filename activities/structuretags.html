What goes in the header? The introductory content for the page, including the site title, logo, and main navigation links.

What goes in the Nav? A section that contains navigation links to other parts of the website or external sites.

Can the Nav go in the header? Yes it can

Does it have to go in the header? Not necessarily. It can even be placed below the header.

Does the Nav contain links to another website? Yes

What goes in Main? The body and the core content of the page>

What goes in the Footer? Copyright and other stuff that contains info about the page's social links, privacy, terms, help and others.
