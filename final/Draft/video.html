Food of the Month: Mango Graham Float

The Mango Graham Float is a popular, no-bake Filipino icebox cake made with layers of crushed graham crackers, whipped cream, and fresh mangoes. It's an easy, refreshing dessert perfect for any occasion! The mango, a native fruit of South Asia, is one of the most widely cultivated fruits in the tropics.
