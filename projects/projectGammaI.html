Top 3 Anime that I'm Always Interested With

Psycho-Pass

A futuristic and philosophical anime that questions the importance of free will. In a dystopian country of Japan, the Sibyl System, a very advanced technology that can read people's mind and stress level was implented. Due to this, peace and order is attained. Also, the people need to be a good citizen, and take care of their stress level to regulate their Psycho-Pass, the data from each person's psychology which determines if one is a healthy citizen, a latent criminal, or someone who isn't needed in the society anymore.

Attack on Titan

Set in a world where humanity is close to extinction, this dark fantasy anime introduces lots of plot twist, action, and suspense. The anime follows Eren Yeager, and his friends Mikasa Ackerman, Armin Arlert. After their hometown was destroyed by Titans, colossal humanoid creatures that devour humans seemingly without reason, Eren commits himself to ridding all of them off. The anime proceeds with all three friends joining the military to fight off the Titans, and discovering where they originated from.

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

It is a fantasy anime that touches on time, friendship, and discovery. A hero's party beat the Demon King with Frieren as one of the elf mages. She finds out that after their triumph that her human friends have a very brief lifetime compared to her and thus goes on a journey to learn about the importance of life and friendships with them. The anime follows her explorations as she meets new individuals, discovers other cultures, and looks back at previous experiences.
