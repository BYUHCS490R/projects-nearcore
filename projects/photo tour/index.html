There are lots of things in the Phillipines that you take with you,the scent of the air after rain, the sound of laughter from a small coastal village, warmth in the smile you encounter. This photo tour acts as a journey to capture that feeling. Each photo is a window into the moments that remind us how alive the world can be with quiet cloud-wrapped volcanoes, ancient streets paused in time, and landscapes that make you relax and breathe. These photographs aren't about seeing. It is about the sense of awe, of stillness, of connection that you experience by seeing more closely.
