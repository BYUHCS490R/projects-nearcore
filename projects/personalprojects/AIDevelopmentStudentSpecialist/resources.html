✅ Good Hands-On Online Training Options

Courses and labs that give practical experience.

Suggested Courses & Labs

Power Automate — Microsoft Learn

Create flows (email attachments, button flows) in guided labs. Free.

Power Automate: Automation Workshop

Hands-on modules: desktop flows, cloud flows. Free / Microsoft workshops.

Alison — Power Automate for Beginners

Learn triggers, actions, and building workflows hands-on. Free.

Caltech — AI Tools for Everyone

On-demand lab style course using AI in content, research, coding, and design. Free options.

Google Cloud — ML & AI Training

Interactive labs, deployable models on Google Cloud. Free and paid tiers.

Coursera — AI For Everyone

Theory & concepts. Free to audit; certificate paid.

edX — Broad AI & ML Courses

Practical projects and coding assignments; some free.

Kaggle Learn

Short, hands-on exercises for data cleaning, pandas, and notebooks — free.

How to Choose & Use Trainings Wisely

  • Start with one tool or domain; don’t try to learn everything at once.
  • Mix guided labs with a self-driven mini-project.
  • Use free tiers and sandboxes to avoid cost barriers.
  • Document what you do and keep a project repo on GitHub.
  • Pair courses with reading on AI ethics (FERPA, GDPR) as you build.
  • Set small milestones (weekly) to keep momentum.
