About the Rankings

The anime chosen for this table represent one of the top-rated or most critically acclaimed titles released in each respective year. Anime is a rapidly growing medium, and each year brings a diverse selection of high-quality shows across many genres, including intense action (*Attack on Titan*), psychological sci-fi (*Cyberpunk: Edgerunners*), and reflective fantasy (*Frieren*).

These rankings often shift based on viewer polls and critical consensus, but the selected anime generally stand out as being highly influential and popular for their respective years.
